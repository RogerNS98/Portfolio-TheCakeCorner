Our History



In the quaint town of Eguisheim, renowned pastry chef Amelia Sugarbaker fulfilled her lifelong dream, opening "The Cake Corner" in 2005. Armed with secret family recipes and a passion for perfection, she transformed a charming corner shop into a haven for delectable delights. Word quickly spread about her mouthwatering creations, drawing locals and visitors alike. With each carefully crafted confection, The Cake Corner became a cherished destination, celebrating joyous occasions and savoring the sweet moments of life.

As the aroma of freshly baked pastries wafted through the air, the quaint town embraced the delightful spirit of The Cake Corner. Amelia's artistic touch and dedication to quality earned her a devoted following. Soon, the little bakery expanded, adding an outdoor terrace adorned with blooming flowers, creating an enchanting atmosphere for patrons to relish their treats. Amelia's passion extended beyond the bakery; she hosted community events, teaching aspiring bakers and organizing festive occasions. The town's heart beat to the rhythm of sugar and flour, as The Cake Corner continued to be a hub of sweetness. Eguisheim, forever marked by the magic of Amelia's confections, became a cherished haven for those seeking a taste of pure joy.
